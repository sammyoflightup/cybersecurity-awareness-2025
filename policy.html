Cyber Policy & User Responsibility

Protecting your privacy. Promoting digital responsibility.

Your Data Is Safe With Us

We take your privacy and digital safety seriously. Any information you share on this platform — whether during training, registration, or communication — is stored securely and is never sold, shared, or exposed to third parties. We only collect what we truly need to serve you better.

Our Promise

  • We do not sell your data to anyone.
  • We do not share your personal information without your permission.
  • We protect your information using safe digital practices and encrypted platforms.
  • We are committed to transparency and legal compliance.

Respecting Nigerian Cyber Laws

We fully comply with the Nigerian Cybercrimes (Prohibition, Prevention, etc.) Act. This law guides how users and admins must behave online to prevent crime, harassment, and abuse. Here’s what every visitor should know:

  • Do not attempt to hack, tamper, or access systems you’re not authorized to use.
  • Do not spread false information or record private conversations without consent.
  • Do not post or share harmful, hateful, or abusive content.
  • If you're part of a group or community we run (e.g. WhatsApp, Telegram), we expect respectful and lawful engagement.
  • Group admins are legally responsible for content posted under their watch.

Our Responsibility to You

We actively protect this platform from abuse. We will never misuse your data, and we are committed to digital education, safety, and empowerment. If you feel unsafe, or if you believe someone is abusing our platform or violating this policy, please report it to us immediately.
